# PRODUCT.md — Raheeq Kanjo Personal Website

## Register
brand — the site IS the product: a personal portfolio/brand site for a writer.

## Product Purpose
A one-page Arabic (RTL) personal website for **رحيق كنجو (Raheek Kanjo)** — Senior Content Writer, Ghostwriter, Storyteller, Scriptwriter based in Tartus, Syria. The site replaces/extends her CV page (raheeqkanjo.surge.sh) and converts LinkedIn visitors and potential clients into inquiries.

## Users
- Arab founders, marketing agencies, and personal-brand clients looking to hire a senior Arabic writer (ghostwriting, LinkedIn content, video scripts).
- Recruiters/collaborators arriving from her LinkedIn (2,842 followers, active Arabic content-writing voice).

## Brand & Tone
- Warm, literary, confident. She writes: "الأدب هو أقصر طريق إلى قلب القارئ".
- Storytelling is the core identity: scripts, narrative, chapters, manuscripts, ink and paper.
- Language: Arabic, RTL, with light English role labels where natural.

## Strategic principles
- Brand fidelity to the existing CV: ivory paper, espresso ink, warm gold, El Messiri + Tajawal.
- The site should feel like a beautifully typeset Arabic manuscript brought to life, not a SaaS template.
- Real numbers as proof: 7+ years, 2M+ campaign views, 12+ managed accounts, 500K+ views for مجتمع مسافة.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages, dark "tech" portfolios, neon gradients, glassmorphism.
- Anything that ignores the ivory/gold CV identity.
